About Fresh Basket

Fresh Basket was founded with a simple mission: to make fresh, high-quality, and organic produce accessible to everyone. We believe that healthy eating should be easy and affordable.

Our journey started in 2020 when we realized the difficulty of finding truly fresh vegetables in the city. We partnered with local farmers to bring their harvest directly to your kitchen, cutting out the middleman and ensuring the best quality for our customers.

Our Mission

To deliver farm-fresh goodness to every household while supporting local agriculture and sustainable farming practices.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and reliable online grocery platform known for quality, freshness, and exceptional service.
